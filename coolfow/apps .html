Our Story

About Cool Flow

Students by day, musicians by night. From the heart of Cebu to your ears.

Who We Are

We are Cool Flow — a group of passionate young musicians from the Philippines, particularly from the beautiful island of Cebu.

We are students of Rosemont Hills Montessori College in Danao City, Cebu. What started as casual jam sessions between classmates has grown into a performing group that brings acoustic warmth to every stage we touch.

We sing regularly at resto bars and on special occasions like weddings, parties, and private events. Our sound is raw, authentic, and deeply rooted in the acoustic tradition — stripped down to vocals and guitars, with every note carrying genuine emotion.

"All the suffering, stress, and addiction comes from not realizing you already are what you are looking for."

— Jon Kabat-Zinn

Our Journey

Cool Flow was formed during our high school years at Rosemont Hills Montessori College. Bonded by a shared love for music, we began performing at school events and local gatherings in Danao City.

As our sound developed, we started booking gigs at resto bars across Cebu, building a loyal following who appreciate our acoustic interpretations of popular hits.

Today, with 4.38K subscribers on YouTube and a growing presence on Facebook and TikTok, we continue to share our music with the world — one cover at a time.
